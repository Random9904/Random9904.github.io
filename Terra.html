Terra

Straight from the land down under― in South Korea. Really makes you think… What beer do you think is the most popular in SK? If you guessed Terra then you have been here before cheater.

They are found all over the place

>Large green beer inflatables on the sidewalk in the youthful Hongdae street
>Stickers on the wall with bright white letters in the dingiest of toilets
>15 second YouTube ads with a Korean man drowning in the golden liquid

Given that it is a beer, depending on the individual(s), the amount needed for a true good buzz varies widely. Sober sallies and frequent designated drivers might fall victim to a single tall boi (500mL). For the true connoisseurs or those wanting of a Korean staple then the mixing of alcohol is recommended. However not just any alcohol, while in SK only soju or the Korean rice spirit will do.

PROTIP #12594737

>Beer glass
>Shot glass
>Soju
>Terra
>Chopsticks

Have your youngest Korean friend pour the table’s shot glasses with soju. Repeat with the beer glasses about 3/4 full of Terra beer. Take your respective shot glass and pour into the beer glass. Grab one chop stick and stick into beer glass. Using the other chop stick, hit the chop stick in the glass with the force of a thousand suns. If done correctly, the drink should bubble up suddenly and calm itself within seconds. Drink as desired
